What does the skill skit do:
This skill kit helps different companies to identify their ideal customer profiles (ICP) from scratch or validate existing ones. An ICP encompasses potential customer segments based on relevant use cases, revenue upside, and pain points that the company can directly solve. 
The skill analyses customer patterns, company data, competitor intelligence (where relevant), to identify the ICPs. Using the identified ICPs and the product/service being offered, the skill will craft product positioning and messaging angles. 
Use this skill when refreshing a stale ICP or when a company isn’t sure who they should be targeting. 
General Instructions
